As we work to become the partner of choice for delivering DBT client outcomes, weâ€™ve made strategic changes to our Delivery model and ways of working that apply new and more skills to support solutions our DBT strategy promises. PS HOW is how we change our ways of working to be more current in lean agile methods, not only for ourselves but for our clients.

  • We are evolving our capabilities by making additions such as Management Consulting that allow us to evaluate our client's business and find sources of value.
  • We are amplifying data and AI skills because data is the fuel to drive everything and needs to be our fuel too.
  • We are advancing the way we have previously thought about creative by shifting less of the way we apply our design thinking to marketing and advertising problems, and more to how we design experiences that motivate human beings associated with the DBT solutions we put in place.
  • Lastly, we are moving away from thinking about technology as a skill, to engineering using technology to produce outcomes.

What Are Crafts?

Crafts represent a set of skills needed for particular roles within the Capability Groups (or CGs)

  • Each craft will be owned/anchored to one of the four CGs.
  • Note, we are retiring Discipline as a construct from our operating model.
  • Craft is one of the three primary axes for performance evaluation (with the others being Client Impact and Core Values).
